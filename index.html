Intro

We decided to make a VR cooking game.
There will be two to three stations that you can cook specific food.
Recipe (HUD) and ingredient will be provided.
The user have to use utensils correctly to corresponding ingredient.
For example, if you pick salt and place it on the steak, it will salt the stake.
However, if you pick a water and place it on the steak, nothing will happen.
